Simple Chinese Chess
Simple PvP hidden-piece Xiangqi variant — 4×8 board
Type the same room code as your friend to join the same room.
Players
Rematch: press once — any player may request rematch.
How to play
  • Click any hidden tile (■) to flip (flip = your turn).
  • Click your revealed piece → click highlighted tile to move (1 square orthogonal).
  • Hidden tiles block movement until flipped.
  • Capture allowed by rank (attacker wins ties). Soldier (兵) can capture General (将); General cannot capture Soldier.
  • First revealed piece sets your color.
