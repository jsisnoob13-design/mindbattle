Mind Battle PvP

Game Rules
  • Both players secretly choose an action.
  • Actions reveal at the same time.
  • First player to lose all HP loses the round.
  • First player to win 10 rounds wins the match.
Actions
  • ⚔ Attack — damage opponent
  • 🛡 Defend — block attack
  • ⚡ Charge — gain energy
  • 💥 Power — strong attack (cost 2 energy)
