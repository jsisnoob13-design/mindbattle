Mind Battle

How to Play
  • Both players secretly choose an action.
  • Actions reveal at the same time.
  • Winner removes one ❤️ from opponent.
  • First player to lose all hearts loses.
Actions
  • 🪨 Rock beats ✂ Scissors
  • ✂ Scissors beats 📄 Paper
  • 📄 Paper beats 🪨 Rock
  • 💥 Gun beats everything
  • Gun has a 3 round cooldown
